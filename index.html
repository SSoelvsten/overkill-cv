Curriculum Vitae
PhD student advised by Prof. Jaco van de Pol researching the theoretical and practical design of logics, algorithms, and data structures for model checking and synthesis of Multi-Agent Systems (MAS).

Tools I have developed as part of my research:
  • Adiar: An I/O efficient implementation of Binary Decision Diagrams (BDDs).

Teaching

I have been a Teaching Assistant in Computability and Logic (spring 2020 and spring 2021) under Prof. Jaco van de Pol, in Foundations of Algorithms and Datastructures (fall 2017) under Assoc. Prof. Kasper Green Larsen, and in Regularity and Automata (spring 2017) under Prof. Anders Møller.

I have also supervised 2 bachelor students as they for a summer project implemented an I/O-efficient Binary Decision Diagram based on my work following up on a paper by Lars Arge in 1996.
